A Soapbox for The Forest.

This is an interactive soapbox that includes 10 sonic audio clips from a little forest beloved by the children, educators and families of St. Johns Childcare, in the hopes to provoke new perspectives on sustainability.

To use the Soapbox press any number on your keyboard and it will play a sound clip from the forest. Press the same number again to stop the audio clip from playing. Each number on your keyboard 0 to 9 corresponds with an audio clip.

This virtual soapbox offers an intervention to slow down and examine what we are sustaining through sustainability. Let the sounds inturrupt you and question if sustainbility has become synonymous with progress and development. Is there any space left for anything else to live and thrive in such worlds?
