About

As an artist and scholar, I employ research and visual design principles to create visually engaging and thought-provoking works that explore complex themes and ideas. My aim is to challenge assumptions and expand our understanding of the world through my art and scholarship.

  • Graphic Design

    In my designs, I prioritize attention to detail, visual communication, and conveying messages in a clear and impactful manner through the strategic use of typography, color, and composition.

  • Research

    My current research centers around experimenting with emerging technologies and art practices to imagine and frame equitable and socially just pedagogical practices.

  • Art & Illustration

    I use art and illustration as a tool for comprehension, to explain complex concepts and phenomena. My visual storytelling conveys information in an engaging and accessible way.

  • Curriculum & Pedagogy

    I approach curriculum and pedagogy with a focus on early childhood education. I seek to learn from children's voices through artistic collaborations and propose practices that support futures yet to come.
